We are now recruiting volunteers for our camps and public drop-in. Don't miss out on science fun for all ages!

We are always looking for volunteers at the BIG Little Science Centre. If you have a couple of hours to donate, please contact us.

Our volunteer opportunities are wide ranging and varied, some examples are:

  • welcoming guests and collecting admission fees from visitors during drop-in times: – Tuesday to Saturday from 10am-4pm
  • showing guests through the hands-on rooms
  • clean-up equipment after class visits and labs – school days
  • conducting science shows – for those who like a leadership role
  • designing science shows – for those who like to be challenged
  • designing and building new displays – for those who are creative
  • marketing and business planning
  • acting as a director in the BIG Little Science Centre Society
